## Sentiment Analysis on Product Quality

**Authors:** Bhumika Jain, Vishwa Patel, Akkshat Shah  
**Course:** Big Data Analytics / Predictive Analytics for Business  
**Tools:** Python, Pandas, Matplotlib, Seaborn, NLTK (if used)

---

## Overview

This project explores whether consumer sentiments about “smartness” features influence the perceived product quality (proxied by review ratings).

We analyze textual reviews and ratings to see if sentiment can predict higher or lower product ratings.

---

## Dataset

Reviews dataset with ratings and textual feedback.

**Features include:**

- Sentiment score  
- Review text  
- Product rating  

---

## Approach

1. Clean and preprocess textual data  
2. Calculate sentiment scores from reviews  
3. Explore relationship between sentiment and product ratings  
4. Visualize trends using plots:  
   - Sentiment vs Rating  
   - Review Distribution  
5. Analyze correlations and insights  

---

## Results & Insights

- Positive sentiment generally correlates with higher ratings  
- Certain “smartness” constructs impact perceived quality more than others  
- Visualizations highlight patterns that could guide product improvements
